Arraybot

Arraybot is a Discord bot written in Java, using the JDA library. The bot's goal is to aid with certain activities, and to customise your Discord experience to your need. Arraybot is capable of running 100% on it's own, all you need to do is add the bot to your guild.

Copycat

The Copycat Discord bot is an advanced say-bot that is designed for flexibility and the ability to hook into other bots. For more information please check out the GitHub page or read the wiki.
